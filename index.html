About ladyfragile-games

ladyfragile-games is an automated content pipeline that generates high-quality gaming videos and publishes them directly to TikTok. Our system captures gameplay footage, generates custom banners, adds voiceover narration, and applies TikTok-optimized overlays — all automatically.

Every video goes through our pipeline: Steam screenshot capture, banner generation with game title and metadata, Spanish voiceover narration, and final composition with a promotional overlay. The result is polished gaming content delivered daily to our TikTok audience.
